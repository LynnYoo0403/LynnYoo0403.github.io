Yiming Liu is a PhD candidate at Southern University of Science and Technology. She obtained her master degree from Bartlett School of Architecture, UCL. Her major research interests focus on the application of intelligent systems to support sustainable architectural design. She is dedicated to bridging the gap between digital design and environmental efficiency, offering data-driven solutions for the contemporary built environment. Her work is published in the leading journal Automation in Construction and presented at a series of international conferences in the CAAD community. She also serves as a reviewer for conferences, including CAADRIA, CAADFutures, CDRF, and xArch.
